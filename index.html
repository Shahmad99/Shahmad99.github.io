Shafiq Ahmad

I completed my PhD in Computer Vision and Deep Learning from PAVIS, Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), Genova, Italy, under the supervion of Dr. Alessio Del Bue and co-supervisor Dr.Pietro Morerio. In my PhD, I tackled privacy concerns in video surveillance, focusing on privacy-preserving person re-identification and human pose estimation with event-based vision systems. Additionally, I created the first-ever event-based dataset for person re-identification.
